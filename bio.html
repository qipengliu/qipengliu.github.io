Biography

Qipeng Liu received the B.S. degree in automation from Jinan University, Jinan, China, in 2007, the M.S. degree in control theory and control engineering from the Jiangsu University of Science and Technology, Zhenjiang, China, in 2009, and the Ph.D. degree in control theory and control engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, in 2014.

From July 2014 to September 2016, he was a lecturer at the Institute of Complexity Science, Qingdao University, Qingdao, China.

He is now a research fellow at the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.
